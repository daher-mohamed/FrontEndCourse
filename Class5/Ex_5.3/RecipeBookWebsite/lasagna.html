Classic Beef Lasagna

A hearty and flavorful lasagna layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk, nutmeg)
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat some olive oil and sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned. Stir in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg until smooth.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta mixture, béchamel sauce, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
